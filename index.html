About Us

Melissa and Dustin met at the University of Virginia in 2018. As fate would have it, they would exchange their first glances to each other through a campus dining hall. Not knowing at the time - that glance would turn into many more experiences and memories for the months to come.

If it weren't for that fateful day, maybe none of this would happen. Maybe - I wouldn't have met this beautiful person and have her be a huge part of my life. I'm glad that I did.
